While reading the first section, you feel engaged by the impact of non natural conditions also known as human impact on animal extinction. The text is very concrete and no further explanations or citations are required for the articles which the author uses as arguments.

When finishing scrolling the interactive tortoise leads you to the next section. All the below sections are more detailed information about each species. All these 3 sections have the same kind of visualization. First of all, you can notice that basically there are 2 colors. Gray scale and red.
